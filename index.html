THE BAND

SUM OF TALENTED PARTS

They say that music is the language of the soul, and nothing could be truer for this Band. Composed of a group of inspirational musicians and skillful performers, the artists behind IKHTIYAAR have earned their reputation as a talented band to look out for. Explore the official site and check out their music below.


Soumyadeep Sikdar


Lead Vocalist

Member since 2017

Being one of the founder members of Ikhtiyaar, he is a strong back up for the band. His melodious and ravishing voice texture has always been a boon for Ikhtiyaar.

Ayush Mishra


Lead Guitarist

Member since 2017

With a rich rock taste, his magical fingers have always set the stage on fire. Entering with the perfect lead and ending with a smooth slide, one cannot take his eyes off him on the stage.

Aniruddha Mondal


Drummer

Member since 2017

Well versed with fusion music, he stuns the crowd by playing drums, cajon and other percussions concurrently. This drummer's presence on the stage is felt with vibrance.

Subhajit Sarkar


Bass Guitarist

Member since 2017

Heavy bass from his five strings adds a groovy texture to their music. He is the one who often comes up with the most unique twist in a song.
